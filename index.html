About Us

Project Hub is a platform designed to showcase student projects from Vignan's Institute of Information Technology. Our mission is to provide a space where students can share their innovative ideas, technical achievements, and creative solutions with the wider community.

Our Community

This website was created by the Advanced Computer Science Engineering (ACSE) department.

Technologies Used

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, with backend integrated via Firebase.

Future Plans

We plan to expand the platform with higher functionality and better user experience.

For queries and suggestions: projecthubqueries@gmail.com

🌟 Our Motive

"Explore innovative projects by students."

We believe the best way to learn is to build — and the best way to inspire is to share. Project Hub puts every student project in one place so juniors learn, seniors are recognised, and great ideas don't get lost in folders.
